# Welcome to Our Research Group

We're excited to launch our new website for the Autonomous Discovery of Alloys (AutoDIAL) research group at the University of Toronto's Department of Materials Science and Engineering.

- **Publications**: New papers and research findings

- **Student Achievements**: Recognizing the accomplishments of our team members

Stay tuned for more exciting updates as we continue our journey in autonomous materials discovery!
